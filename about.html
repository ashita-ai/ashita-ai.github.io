About

The difference isn't the tech. It's the decisions made in the first year that compound for the next five.

I'm Evan Volgas. I've spent 20+ years building data and distributed systems across Netflix, Mailchimp, Capital One, and a handful of startups. I'm currently working on AI-enabled data engineering and wealth tech.

I have seen startups and data teams fail. I have seen them succeed. The pattern is consistent: pilots that never reach production, architecture decisions that haunt you, healthy metrics on broken systems. The technology usually works. The problem is everything else.

I write about what I've learned. I also build tools that didn't survive corporate roadmaps but deserved to exist anyway.

If you want to talk about data platforms, AI systems, or the decisions that matter before the code does, get in touch.
